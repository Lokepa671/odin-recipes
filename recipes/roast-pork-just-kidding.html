Roast Pork, Just Kidding!

Description

This recipe is from an anime called Food War. This dish is an western style cuisine type and invented by Soma. This recipe was served to a urban residential planner to help save Soma's family restaurant from being shut down. The primary ingredients for this dish are potatoes, bacon, and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • 6 white potatoes
  • Eringi mushrooms
  • 1 large white onion, or 2 medium white onions
  • 1 spring of rosemary
  • Butter (min 2 tablespoons)
  • 2 packs of thick-cut bacon
  • 1 cup, or 250 ml of red wine
  • 1/4 cup, or 60ml of sake (Any sake that is labeled "sweet")
  • 2 tbs, or 30 ml of soy sauce
  • 1 spring of parsley or any garnish

Step by Step

  1. Peel the Potatoes

    2. -Amount: 6 White Potatoes

  2. Slice the Potatoes

    3. -Wash the potatoes then cut them into quarters or halves, depending on size.

  3. Chop the Mushrooms and Onions into Equally-size Cubes

    4. -Amount: 3 Eringi mushrooms and 1 large white onion or 2 medium white onions.

  4. Steam the Potatoes

    5. -Steam potatoes until soft (about 15-20 minutes).

    -DO NOT steam or microwave the mushrooms.

  5. Pan Fry the Onions and Mushrooms

    6. Prep the pan with a tablespoon of butter.

    -Pan fry onions and mushrooms until caramelized or fully golden.

  6. Mash the Potatoes

    7. -Mash into small chunks in a large bowl. Do not mash too much, you want to avoid it being frothy to form it to shape.

  7. Combine the Onions and Mushrooms, Mix with the Potato Mash

    8. -Mix until the ingredients are even in the bowl.

  8. Pick the Rosemary

    9. -Pick the rosemary off its stem.

    -Set aside.

  9. Add the Salt

    10. -Add a few sprinkles of salt to the top of the bowl.

  10. Form the Fake Roast

    11. -Let the mix cool until touchable (more than 5 minutes). CAUTION HOT -Take all of the mix and form it into a log shape.

  11. Wrap the Roast in Bacon

    12. -Amount: 2 packs of thick-cut bacon.

    -Wrap the roast fully, try not to leave any gaps, otherwise, the potatoes will seep out when the bacon tightens.

    -There is no surefire way of doing this, just coat it to the best of your ability.

  12. Tie the Roast

    13. -Tie the roast with the twine. It doesn’t need to be anything fancy, just wrap it around a few times.

    -Intertwine the rosemary afterward.

  13. Bake in the Oven

    14. -Bake at 375ºF or 190ºC for 30-45 minutes or until bacon is golden (cook longer for crispy bacon), turning halfway.

  14. Boil the Wine In the Frying Pan

    15. -Amount: 1 Cup, or 250ml.

    -Bring the wine to a boil (~10 minutes) on medium-high heat in a frying pan. The aim is to reduce it.

  15. Add the Butter , Sake, and Soy Sauce to the Wine

    16. -Amount: 1 tbs of butter, 1/4 cup of sake, 2 tbs of soy sauce.

    -Melt the butter into the boiling wine.

    -Pour the soy sauce and sake into the sauce.

    -Continue to cook for 2-3 minutes.

  16. Remove the Twine and the Rosemary

    17. -When the baking is done, remove the twine and rosemary, they are not to be eaten.

  17. Pour the Sauce on the Roast

    18. -Pour the finished sauce onto the roast (make sure the roast is on a plate to hold the extra sauce). Pour slowly.

  18. Add the Garnish

    19. -Add a garnish to the top of the dish, we used parsley, the anime used watercress.

  19. Serve
